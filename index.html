Guessing Dota 2 Heroes

High Score: 0

Rules:

The goal of the game is to correctly guess as many heroes as possible in 100 seconds.

For each question, the primary attribute and the first ability of the hero will be revealed.

The second, third, and ultimate abilities will be revealed consecutively after 3, 6, and 9 seconds.

After 12 seconds, the image of the hero will be shown.

You can skip the question after 7 seconds from when the question appears.

Point Calculation:

  • < 6 seconds: +8 points
  • 6-11 seconds: +5 points
  • > 11 seconds: +3 points
